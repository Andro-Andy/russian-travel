Traveling in Russia

The real country is not in the news releases, but here.

What didn't we see there's?

According to ВЦИОМ polls, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% plan to spend their holidays in their native country. Like, what have we not seen here at home? In fact, Russia is a whole universe with the gentle sea of ​​the south, thick Sayan forests and severe ice of the Putorana Plateau. And you can also see all these beauties without millions in the account, a passport and multi-hour flights. Like, for example, Vera Bashmakova, a brave young mother who took three children in her arms and sat them in her "Lado" and traveled 20 thousand kilometers in her native country. We have selected and described some interesting places worthy of your vacation.

Almost the entire peninsula is located beyond the Arctic Circle. Saami tundra, from which to the south is taiga, and to the north is the Arctic Ocean, pretending to be the Barents Sea.

Perhaps you watched Zvyagintsev and even heard the story of the Arctic festival in Teriberka. Perhaps the word "Khibiny" did not remain under the snow of school memories of geography lessons. Perhaps you were not interested in the ultra-deep well penetrating the earth's crust, but you have long been away from apatites covers apathy. But your dream of seeing the northern lights comes true with a ticket to Murmansk.

Altai

URL

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. First of all, because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes, strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in the car, you will be able to get acquainted with the invisible miracle of these places - mountain air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you will see all the variety of local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai, Eagles fly over the ridges, and roe deer graze on the plains. And the famous manuls - too inhabitants of the Altai Territory.

Winter Baikal

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many also know that this is the largest source of fresh water and one of the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course, it's all true. But Baikal is still an ideal place for competitions in skiing. This is a kind of sport when a skier ties himself to a motorcycle, and the tandem tries to develop as much as possible more ice speed. In March 2019, a world record was set at the Baikal Mile festival - 197.011 km/h

Karelia

URL

http://vodlozero.ru

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian taiga that forms larch does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here it towers up to 30 meters - the forests of Karelian national parks due to impenetrable swamps never knew the axe. Some pines are more than half a millennium old. Touch a living creature that saw the sun before Ivan the Terrible saw it. In the virgin forest for a hundred kilometers you will not find a path. And on rare paths trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. Let everyone know who's boss.
